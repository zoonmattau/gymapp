7. How Artificial Intelligence Uses Your Data

Plain-English Explanation of Our AI

UpRep uses AI to make your fitness experience smarter. When you log a workout, our system analyses your history to suggest better plans, flag plateaus, and personalise your next session. This is done automatically by software — not by a human coach. It is a tool to support your training, not clinical or medical advice.

7.1 What our AI does with your data

  • Analyses your workout history to identify trends, plateaus, and areas for improvement
  • Generates personalised workout plan suggestions based on your goals, history, and fitness level
  • Produces progress insights and milestone notifications
  • May adjust recommendation difficulty based on performance patterns over time

7.2 What our AI does NOT do

  • It does not provide medical diagnoses, treatment plans, or clinical health assessments
  • It does not share your individual data with external AI companies or model-training platforms
  • It does not make automated decisions that have legal or similarly significant effects on you
  • It is not a substitute for advice from a qualified personal trainer, physiotherapist, or physician

7.3 Automated Decision-Making

Our AI features generate suggestions and insights automatically, without human review of each individual output. These are informational tools only and do not constitute decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects on you. No UpRep AI feature will:

  • determine your eligibility for any service, insurance, employment, or financial product
  • share your data with third parties for profiling or targeting purposes
  • make any binding decision about you without a human being able to review it

You have the right to request human review of any AI-generated output that you believe is inaccurate or harmful by contacting privacy@uprep.com.au.

7.4 Your rights around AI personalisation

  • You may opt out of AI-powered personalisation at any time via Settings > Privacy in the app
  • If you opt out, the core workout logging features remain fully available — only personalised AI suggestions are disabled
  • We use aggregated, de-identified data to improve our AI models over time. Your individual data is never identifiable in this process.

12. How We Protect Your Information

The security of your personal information is a priority for us. We implement layered technical and organisational safeguards:

12.1 Technical safeguards

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt or Argon2 — they are never stored in plain text and cannot be read by our team
  • Access to production systems and user databases is restricted to authorised personnel only, using role-based access controls
  • We use multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all administrative access to production systems
  • Regular automated vulnerability scanning and penetration testing is conducted on the Platform

12.2 Organisational safeguards

  • Only team members who genuinely need access to personal data are granted it (principle of least privilege)
  • All personnel with access to personal data are bound by confidentiality obligations
  • We review our security posture regularly and update practices in response to emerging threats

12.3 Data breach response

Despite our best efforts, no system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach:

  • We will investigate and contain the breach as rapidly as possible
  • Where the breach is likely to result in serious harm to any individual, we will notify the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) and affected users within 30 days, in accordance with the Notifiable Data Breaches (NDB) scheme
  • Notifications will include a description of what happened, what data was involved, what steps we are taking, and what you can do to protect yourself
  • Under the NDB scheme, we must notify the OAIC and affected individuals as soon as practicable — typically within 30 days of becoming aware of an eligible breach
  • We will never charge you for notifications or for steps taken to help you following a breach
  • We maintain an incident response plan that is reviewed annually

A Note on Absolute Security

While we take all reasonable precautions, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we commit to responding promptly and transparently if something goes wrong.
